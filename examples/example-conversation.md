**Breakdown:**

- **DE**: From  
- **KN**: Invite only the called station to reply  
- **RST**: Signal report (599 / 579)  
- **QTH**: Location (Oslo / London)  
- **LOC**: Maidenhead grid locator  
- **QSL?**: Asking for confirmation  
- **SK / 73**: End of contact and best regards
